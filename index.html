How It Works

Three seamless steps — from raw sensor data to clear, actionable reports on your dashboard.

1

Data Capture

IoT cameras and microphones embedded in the classroom continuously capture facial expressions, body language, and spoken audio — all processed locally for maximum privacy.

2

AI Analysis

Deep learning models analyze attention levels, detected emotions, speech patterns, and participation rates in real time — producing per-student engagement scores every 30 seconds.

3

Teacher Insights

Teachers receive beautifully visualised, easy-to-read engagement reports on their dashboard — with natural language queries, trend analysis, and personalised recommendations.
